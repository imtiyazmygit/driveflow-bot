# Client Instructions

## Welcome
This portal is used to submit UK driving test booking requests.

## What the client needs to do
1. Open the booking portal in your browser.
2. Log in using the provided username and password.
3. Select your preferred test centre from the dropdown list.
4. Enter your email address so you can receive status updates.
5. Click the Submit Booking Request button.

## What happens after submission
- Your request will be sent to the owner/operator for processing.
- The owner will run the booking automation from the backend.
- You will receive updates by email once the status changes.

## Important note
- The client does not need to access the source code.
- The client only uses the portal to submit booking requests.
